## Cuisine Styles

**Headline:**
> Choose Your Cuisine — We Build the Experience

**Intro:**
Every cuisine style below comes with a bespoke menu proposal, not a template. We'll match the style to your setting, season, and group preferences.

---

### Mediterranean
The foundation of everything we cook. Sun-dried, sea-salted, and rooted in Greek tradition — then refined with the eye of a contemporary kitchen. Think grilled octopus, hand-rolled dolmades, seared branzino with horta, and desserts built around Aegean honey and seasonal citrus.

### Nikkei
Japanese-Peruvian fusion at its most considered. Delicate tiraditos, tightly rolled nigiri, leche de tigre ceviches, and robata-grilled skewers — a cuisine that demands precision and rewards it. This is the menu for guests who appreciate something genuinely unexpected.

### Italian
Handmade pasta. Slow-cooked ragù. Risotto stirred to order. Italian food, done with respect for the classics and an eye for the ingredient. No shortcuts — the kind of meal that feels like it took days, even when it didn't.

### French
From bistro comfort to haute cuisine finesse. Consommés, confit, soufflés, and sauces that take time — this is the cuisine of patience, technique, and the kind of dining room silence that tells you something extraordinary just arrived at the table.

### Japanese
Omakase sensibility without the rigidity. Delicate dashi broths, precisely sliced sashimi, tamago cooked to a specific sweetness, and izakaya plates designed for sharing and conversation. A cuisine of restraint, applied with intention.

### Mexican
Not Tex-Mex. Real Mexican — slow-braised meats, corn-based masa, fresh-made salsas with real heat, and aguachile that arrives cold and leaves an impression. Bold, generous, and built for a table that wants to eat well and linger.

### Asian Fusion
A fluid, ingredient-led journey through East and Southeast Asian cuisine. Thai-inspired larbs, Korean BBQ influences, wok-fried rice that hits properly, and fresh spring rolls with homemade nuoc cham. A sharing-style experience that works beautifully for groups.

### Chef's Table
No cuisine brief. No restrictions. Just a conversation about what you love, the setting you're in, and what we've been excited about lately. The Chef's Table format is a fully bespoke multi-course degustation — the closest thing to a private restaurant experience, in your own space.
