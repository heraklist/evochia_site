## Event Types

### Weddings & Celebrations

A wedding menu should feel like it was designed for this couple, this setting, and this evening — because it was. We work with you from early planning through to the final course, designing a service format (plated, buffet, shared, or mixed) that fits your venue, your guests, and the flow of your day.

Whether it's a courtyard reception, a clifftop dinner, or an intimate villa gathering — we handle every culinary detail with the precision this occasion deserves.

*Formats: Plated · Buffet · Finger food & canapés · Cocktail reception · Mixed format*

---

### Baptisms & Family Events

Family celebrations call for warmth, generosity, and food that brings people together. We design menus that work across generations — from children's portions to elderly guests, dietary needs to regional preferences — without sacrificing quality on any plate.

*Includes: Coffee and pastry arrivals · Buffet or plated mains · Dessert station options*

---

### Corporate Events

First impressions at corporate events are made before anyone speaks. The quality of the food, the precision of the service, the professionalism of the setup — it all signals something about the company hosting it. We understand that, and we execute accordingly.

From working lunches and board-level dinners to conference catering and company celebrations — on time, on brief, presented to a standard that reflects well on your brand.

*Formats: Working lunch · Cocktail reception · Plated dinner · Coffee break station · Multi-day conference*

---

### Themed Experiences

Some events call for more than good food — they call for a concept. A Greek meze night under the stars. A fireside BBQ on the terrace. A sushi counter set up in a private villa. A street food evening for a corporate team-build.

We design, source, and execute complete themed dining experiences — cuisine, presentation, serviceware, and atmosphere, all aligned.

*Themes: Pizza Night · BBQ & Live Grill · Sushi Counter · Greek Meze · Street Food Market · Mexican Fiesta*

---

### Children's Parties

A children's party deserves exactly the same level of thought as any adult event — just applied differently. We design menus that are genuinely appealing to children (not just nominally 'kid-friendly'), presented in a way that creates excitement, and prepared to the highest food hygiene standards.

From themed party food stations and interactive elements to elegant setups that work for both children and the adults at the same event — we make sure everyone at the table is well looked after, regardless of age.

*Formats: Party food stations · Mini buffet · Themed setups · Sweet tables · Combined adult & children's menus*
