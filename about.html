About Evochia

Empowered by Craft, Driven by Taste

A premium catering house built on quality, consistency, and a deep respect for ingredients.

Our Story

Where Tradition Meets Contemporary Craft

Evochia was founded in 2025 by Executive Chef Heraklis, bringing over 12 years of culinary expertise from leading luxury hospitality venues across Greece.

The name Evochia (Ευωχία) means 'a feast' in Greek - the art of gathering around a table with abundance, joy, and intention. It's not just a meal; it's the experience of sharing something meaningful.

Rooted in Mediterranean tradition with a passion for global gastronomy - from Nikkei and Japanese to Italian, French, and Mexican - we bring world-class dining directly to your most cherished spaces.

Our Philosophy

Quality, Taste, and Value - in That Order

We don't chase trends or cut corners. Every menu starts with the best seasonal ingredients, every recipe is tested for balance and consistency, and every service is planned with military-level precision.

The result? Cuisine that speaks for itself and an experience your guests will remember long after the evening ends.
