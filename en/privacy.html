Transparency & Privacy

Privacy Policy

How we handle your data and respect your privacy.

Who We Are

This website is operated by Evochia, a premium event catering and private chef service based in Athens, Greece. For any privacy-related inquiries, contact us at info@evochia.gr.

Cookies We Use

We use a minimal set of cookies:

Cookie Provider Purpose Duration
cc_cookie evochia.gr Stores your cookie consent preferences 182 days
_ga Google Analytics Distinguishes unique visitors 2 years
_gid Google Analytics Distinguishes unique visitors 24 hours

The _ga and _gid cookies are only set if you accept analytics cookies. They are automatically deleted if you later withdraw consent.

Google Analytics

We use Google Analytics 4 (GA4) to understand how visitors interact with our website, such as which pages are visited and how long users stay. This helps us improve our services.

GA4 operates in Consent Mode v2: analytics tracking is completely blocked until you explicitly consent. No data is collected before your approval.

We do not use advertising cookies, and no personal data is shared with third parties for marketing purposes.

How to Opt Out

You can manage your cookie preferences at any time by clicking the link in the footer of any page, or by using the cookie banner that appears on your first visit.

You can also block cookies entirely via your browser settings. Note that the cc_cookie (consent preference) is strictly necessary and cannot be disabled while using the consent feature.

Your Rights (GDPR)

Under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), you have the right to:

  • Access — request a copy of any personal data we hold about you
  • Rectification — ask us to correct inaccurate data
  • Erasure — request deletion of your personal data
  • Portability — receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format
  • Withdraw consent — at any time, without affecting the lawfulness of prior processing

Contact

For any privacy-related requests or questions, please contact us at info@evochia.gr.
