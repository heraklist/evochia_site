Corporate Hospitality

The food should elevate the room without ever disturbing its rhythm.

Corporate hospitality is most powerful when it feels assured rather than overstated. The table should support the occasion, not compete with it.

A private client dinner asks for a different tone from a networking reception; a launch asks for a different cadence from a leadership retreat or multi-day hosted stay.

Our role is to make the hospitality feel polished, capable, and effortless, so the room carries exactly the impression it should.

What's Included

Operational clarity behind the hospitality

The visible part is the table and service. Behind it sits event-appropriate menu planning, staffing, timing, and on-site execution built around the run of the event.

  • Menu planning for receptions, seated dinners, canapes, launches, and longer-format corporate hospitality
  • Dietary planning for mixed guest groups, executive hosting, and allergy-aware service
  • Guidance on service style based on venue layout, guest count, and event tone
  • Coordination with planners, venues, production teams, and schedule owners where needed
  • On-site execution, staff management, and post-service reset with minimal friction
Coverage Across the Event

Formats that support different corporate settings

Networking Reception & Canapes

Food for networking should move easily through the room, feel polished, and support conversation rather than slow it down.

Private Business Dinner

A more contained dinner format works when the event needs discretion, a steadier pace, and a hospitality style that feels elevated without becoming theatrical.

Product Launch & Brand Hospitality

Launches and brand events often need food that looks clean, photographs well, and fits the production rhythm of the schedule.

Multi-Day Corporate Hospitality

For retreats, hosted stays, or multi-day activations, the hospitality can be structured as one coherent plan instead of isolated catering moments.

How We Work

Clear structure behind hospitality that needs to look effortless

The best corporate catering is measured as much by timing, discretion, and calm execution as by what is served on the plate.

The process

01

Brief & event context

We begin with the guest profile, event type, venue, schedule, and the tone the hospitality needs to carry.

02

Menu & service proposal

The proposal aligns the menu, service format, staffing, and timing realities in one practical structure.

03

Execution around the run sheet

Once confirmed, the food and service are built around the event schedule so the hospitality supports the programme instead of competing with it.

Can you adapt to venues with schedule or production constraints?

Yes. The service plan adapts to timing windows, access, setup rules, and venue realities.

Can the service cover both discreet dinners and larger receptions?

Yes. We can structure hospitality for executive dinners, client events, launches, and broader networking formats.

Can you handle mixed dietary preferences?

Yes. Vegetarian, vegan, gluten-free, and allergy-aware requirements are integrated into the planning rather than left as last-minute adjustments.

Planning a corporate event in Greece?

Share the date, guest profile, and format of the event. We will return with a proposal shaped to the tone of the room and the level of hospitality it deserves.
