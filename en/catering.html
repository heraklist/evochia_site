Corporate Events

From working lunches and board-level dinners to conference catering and company celebrations — on time, on brief, and presented to a standard that reflects well on your brand.

What's Included

Everything Taken Care Of

When you work with Evochia, the food is the visible part. Behind it is the structure that holds the experience together — sourcing, prep, staffing, timing, and coordination — so everything feels seamless from your perspective.

  • Custom menu design with dietary accommodations (vegetarian, vegan, gluten-free, allergies)
  • Premium seasonal ingredient sourcing from trusted Greek suppliers
  • Full production: prep, cooking, plating, and service on location
  • On-site setup and service flow shaped around your venue
  • Equipment, serving ware, and disposables (as agreed in proposal)
  • Staffing coordination for larger events
  • Post-event cleanup — we leave your space exactly as we found it
  • Full written proposal with itemised costing before commitment
How We Work Together

From First Message to Final Course

1. Get in Touch — Share the basics: date, guest count, setting, and what you're envisioning. We respond within 24 hours.

2. Consultation — We talk through your vision, preferences, and the practical details of the event. No commitment required at this stage.

3. Tailored Proposal — You receive a detailed written offer: menu, style of service, timeline, and clear pricing.

4. Confirmation — Once confirmed, we handle everything, so you can step back and focus on your guests.
