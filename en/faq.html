Your Questions

Common Questions, Clear Answers

If you don't find what you're looking for here, reach out directly — we're happy to answer anything.

We operate across all of Greece. Our primary areas are Athens and Attica, the Saronic Gulf islands, the Cyclades (Mykonos, Santorini, Paros, Naxos, and others), the Ionian islands, and the broader Greek mainland. If you're hosting an event at a specific location and are unsure whether we cover it, reach out — we'll confirm directly.

For weddings and large events, we recommend booking at least 6–8 weeks in advance. For private chef dinners and smaller events, 2–3 weeks is typically sufficient. During peak summer months (June–September), earlier bookings are strongly advised as availability fills quickly.

For our private chef service, we work with as few as 2 guests. For full event catering, we generally start from 20 guests, though we evaluate smaller events on a case-by-case basis. There is no upper limit — we have the team and logistics to scale to larger celebrations.

Yes, always. When we receive your brief, we ask for a full list of dietary requirements across all guests — allergies, intolerances, vegetarian, vegan, gluten-free, and any other preferences. Menus are built around the guest profile, not the other way around. We never serve a workaround; we design the right course.

Yes. We arrive fully self-sufficient: professional cooking equipment, service ware, mise en place, and everything needed to run a clean, efficient kitchen in your venue. We bring our own team — chef, sous chef (for larger events), and service staff as needed. You do not need to arrange anything culinary-related.

We don't publish fixed prices because every event is different — guest count, menu complexity, location, service hours, and staffing all affect the final figure. Once you share your brief, we prepare a clear, itemised proposal with no hidden costs. There are no surprises after you sign.

The private chef service is designed for intimate settings — typically 2 to 16 guests. The chef works in your kitchen (villa, yacht, apartment) and prepares a multi-course tasting menu tailored to your group. It is a personal, immersive experience with direct interaction if desired. Catering is a full production service for larger events, with a complete front-of-house team, setup logistics, and coordinated service throughout the event.

Yes. Every menu is built from scratch around your occasion, preferences, and guest profile. We present initial direction based on the season and your brief, and we refine from there. You can choose from Mediterranean, Nikkei, or Asian Fusion as a primary direction — or request a combination. Nothing is standardised.

Our scope is culinary — food, service, kitchen logistics, and timing within the event. For full event coordination (florals, venue dressing, entertainment, planning), we work alongside your event planner or wedding coordinator. If you don't have one, we can recommend trusted partners we've worked with.

We respond within 24 hours with questions, initial menu direction, and the beginning of a structured proposal.
