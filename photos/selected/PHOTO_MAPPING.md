## Selection principles

- Primary images should feel premium, editorial, and service-aware.
- Secondary/support images should add texture: canapes, cocktails, close-up details, atmosphere.
- Avoid casual, touristy, buffet-hall, or menu-catalog images as main section visuals.
- When one image must carry a whole category, prefer a photo that signals both `hospitality` and `food`, not decor alone.
